Ruozhen (Rozen) Zheng

Now, I am a supply chain management trainee at DJI. At the same time, I am looking for a PhD position in 2026.
Previously, I received my master degree in MSE and bachelor degree in IMS, both with honors at Shenzhen University, supervised by Prof. Chen Yang

I firmly believe that recommender systems can serve as friendly companions and guides, rather than mere feeding machines that only focus on user clicks. To this end, my current research focus revolves around next-generation recommender systems, e.g., conversational recommenders, with emphasis on (1) accuracy, (2) explainability and (3) interactivity.

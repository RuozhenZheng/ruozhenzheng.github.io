Ruozhen (Rozen) Zheng

Ruozhen (Rozen) Zheng

I am a supply chain management trainee at DJI Technology.
Previously, I received my master degree in Management Science and Engineering (MSE) and bachelor degree in Information Management and Information System (IMIS), both with honors at Shenzhen University, supervised by Prof. Chen Yang.

My previous research contributions include: (1) Inferring User Preferences and Interactions from Data; (2) Supporting recommendation with personalized machine learning; (3) Optimizing multi-constraint problems with Evolutionary Computation. Previously, I have largely viewed personalized recommendation as a ‘black-box’task. This black-box view, while effective for building accurate models, ignores the potential real-world consequences of how such models are applied. There are further issues, such as filter bubbles, diversity, bias and fairness. I would like to present strategies to address them, in order to build personalized models that are more diverse, unbiased, and fair in the context of different application.
